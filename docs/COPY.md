## 11. Brand Voice 品牌聲音

### 溝通原則（中文）

1. 精確描述技術細節，不使用空洞的行銷詞彙。
2. 保持中立且專業的語氣，不使用情緒化字眼。
3. 結構化呈現資訊，優先使用條列式。
4. 強調長期維護性與信賴關係的建立。
5. 不使用任何表情符號或多餘裝飾符號。
6. 雙語對照需保持語意精確，不使用自動翻譯感文字。

### Communication Rules（English）

1. Be precise with technical details; avoid hollow marketing fluff.
2. Maintain a neutral and professional tone; no emotional language.
3. Use structured information; prioritize bullet points.
4. Emphasize long-term maintainability and the building of trusted partnerships.
5. No emojis or superfluous decorative symbols.
6. Ensure bilingual accuracy; avoid "machine-translated" phrasing.

### Preferred Terms 推薦用語

架構 (Architecture) · 模組化 (Modular) · 可維護性 (Maintainability) · 擴展性 (Scalability) · 工程導向 (Engineering-driven) · 結構化 (Structured) · 解耦 (Decoupling) · 效能 (Performance) · 長期資產 (Long-term Asset) · 信賴 (Trust) · 夥伴 (Partnership) · 演進 (Evolution)

### Forbidden Terms 禁止用語

~~魔法 (Magic)~~ · ~~最好 (Best)~~ · ~~簡單 (Simple)~~ · ~~輕鬆 (Easy)~~ · ~~隨便 (Whatever)~~ · ~~漂亮 (Pretty)~~ · ~~萬能 (Ultimate)~~ · ~~廉價 (Cheap)~~ · ~~快速套版 (Template)~~ · ~~黑科技 (Dark Tech)~~

## 12. FAQ 常見問題

### Q1

- **中文：** 你們如何確保長期合作的技術品質？
- **English:** How do you ensure technical quality over long-term engagements?

**Answer (ZH):** 我們在每個專案中建立完整的技術架構文件與模組化設計，確保系統能隨業務演進持續調整。長期合作讓我們對客戶的技術脈絡有更深的理解，進而提供更精準的技術決策支援。

**Answer (EN):** We establish comprehensive architecture documentation and modular design in every project, ensuring systems can adapt as the business evolves. Long-term engagement deepens our understanding of each client's technical context, enabling more precise decision support over time.

---

### Q2

- **中文：** 你們的合作模式是什麼？
- **English:** What does your engagement model look like?

**Answer (ZH):** 我們以夥伴關係為核心，從初期探索到系統上線後的持續支援，全程緊密協作。隨著合作深化，我們對客戶業務的理解也持續累積，成為長期可信賴的技術夥伴。

**Answer (EN):** Our model is built on partnership — from initial discovery through post-launch support, we collaborate closely at every stage. As the relationship deepens, our understanding of the client's business grows, making us an increasingly trusted technical partner.

---

### Q3

- **中文：** 專案開發週期通常多久？
- **English:** How long does a typical project take?

**Answer (ZH):** 依據複雜程度，標準企業官網系統通常需要 8 到 12 週，包含架構設計、開發、測試與正式上線。

**Answer (EN):** Depending on complexity, a standard corporate website system typically takes 8 to 12 weeks, including architecture design, development, testing, and launch.
